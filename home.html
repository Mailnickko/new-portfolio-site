About

Who I am

I'm a full stack Javascript developer, fascinated by technology and the web. I began programming as a creative outlet and was immediately impressed by the community's willingness to educate each other. The problem-solving nature of the field is what ultimately drew me in and I haven't looked back since. When I'm not working on a personal project or peeking into documentation, I enjoy reading up on tech news or listening to podcasts.

What I'm about

I'm always looking for ways to grow my skill sets. Whether that involves consuming the docs of a new framework or deep-diving into a foreign codebase, the amount of knowledge out there keeps me going. I bear a particular fondness for incorporating front end technologies, specifically React + Redux and Angular. I enjoy the power those tools provide in increasing productivity and the medium it creates to deliver that perfect User Experience.

Underneath the title of Software Engineer, however, I believe writing code simply boils down to solving problems methodically, using the right tools, and researching how to use those tools more efficiently. If you share the same passions as I do I'd love to hear from you!
